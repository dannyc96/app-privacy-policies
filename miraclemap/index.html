Privacy Policy — MiracleMap

Last updated: June 26, 2026

MiracleMap ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Daniel Chambers. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account (via Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, or email/password).
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (screens viewed, features used) collected via PostHog analytics.
  • Purchase history: Subscription status managed via RevenueCat. We never see your payment card details — those go directly to Apple.
  • Crash reports: Anonymized crash logs collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app
  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To restore your subscription across devices
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and errors

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and crash reporting — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • PostHog: Product analytics — posthog.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Supabase: Database and backend — supabase.com/privacy

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

MiracleMap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at dannyc.devops@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
